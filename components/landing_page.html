Security Alert

Beware of Fake Calls/Emails: Instances have come to notice that persons are getting calls/emails asking to deposit money in individual bank accounts, claiming Customs have stopped clearance of gifts unless money is deposited.

Customs Department never asks for deposits in individual bank accounts. Do not respond to fake calls.
