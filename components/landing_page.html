Protect Yourself From Fraud

Instances have come to the notice that number of persons are getting telephonic calls as well as e-mail and letters by post asking them to deposit money in individual bank accounts making claim that Customs Officer have stopped clearance/ release of gift parcels/ rewards unless a certain amount is deposited.

The General Public is hereby notified & forewarned not to respond to such fake calls/ mail. Customs Department is not responsible for any such fraudulent incidents.
